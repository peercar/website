Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Peercar ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our peer-to-peer car sharing platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Driver's license and identity verification documents
  • Payment and billing information
  • Vehicle information (for car owners)
  • Location data when using our services
  • Communications with us and other users

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Verify your identity and eligibility to use our platform
  • Connect car owners with renters
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Ensure the safety and security of our community

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users as necessary to facilitate bookings
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • With insurance partners to provide coverage
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and the safety of our users

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of certain communications. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@peercar.rent.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: info@peercar.rent
